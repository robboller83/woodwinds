About Us

I am a passionate traditional player of this beautiful instrument played for over 55,000 years by the Australian Aboriginal People. Known as the Didgeridoo, I have been playing for over 20 years and have been teaching for over 10 years. I have been teaching people from all walks of life and all ages. Now I am looking to teach you how to play this beautiful instrument.
